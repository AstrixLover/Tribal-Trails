# Echoes of the Earth: A Journey into India's Tribal Heritage

This project is a vibrant and immersive web experience dedicated to celebrating the rich, diverse, and ancient cultures of the tribal communities of India. It aims to showcase their history, art, traditions, and way of life through a modern and engaging interface. From the intricate Warli paintings of Maharashtra to the rhythmic Dhemsa dance of Odisha, this site serves as a digital museum and a tribute to the subcontinent's indigenous soul.
